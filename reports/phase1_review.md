## Phase 1 Finding

The selected bug is now supported by three evidence layers:

1. Graph evidence: the graph marks `print_final_scores(...)` in step2 and step3 as reading global `score` while accepting `final_score`.
2. Source evidence: both functions print and branch using `score`, not `final_score`.
3. Behavioral probe: with global `score=0`, non-zero `final_score` values are ignored in observed output.

## Known Limitations
